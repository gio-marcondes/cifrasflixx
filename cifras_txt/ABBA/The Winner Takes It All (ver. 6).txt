TAB:

The original is played in Gb, but I made it G to simplify things a bit. Note that
the Intro, Chorus and Outro keeps this rhythm:
 
G (1234 12)  B7/D# (34)  Em (1234 12)  E7/G# (34)
Am (1234 1234)  D (1234 1234)
 
It will be easier to understand how to play if you keep that pattern in mind.
Listen to the song to get the exact pattern, and enjoy!
