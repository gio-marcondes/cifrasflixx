I feel like a lot of people that are making these for MJ songs tend to try and over complicate
 them with a
bunch of intricate chords that weren't actually there. Michael Jackson songs are generally 
pretty simple. This
is based off the piano and voice only version of the song. The guitar is basically following 
the chord changes
of the piano exactly. The voice is the star here, have fun!
 
*NOTE* the Badd9 and Cadd9 are just a "Bb" and "C" major bar chord played on the A string. The
 chord diagrams
that UG gives aren't the fingerings I am using, so I've posted mine below. The rest of the 
chord diagrams,
however, are correct.
