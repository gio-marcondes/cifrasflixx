Guns N' Roses - If the World
------------------------------
Key of F
Ear-transcribed on ukulele by Neil Killer.
------------------------------
This is my acoustic interpretation of the song. The actual song consists of
various electric guitar riffs and a lot of alternating F and Dm chords.
However, the whole song seems to follow the F - A# - C - Dm progression, even
though the actual chord progression is never played. You can play this
progression while listening to the song and it will fit for the ENTIRE song.
 
There might be some variations of the chord progression that would also fit,
such as replacing A# with Am, but the one I used harmonizes best with the song.
