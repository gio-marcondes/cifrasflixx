Done With Bonaparte Cifra
Mark Knopfler

Afinação: E A D G B E
Capotraste: 5th traste

TAB:

This version of "Done With Bonaprte" is based on the performance at the 2007 AVO 
Session which was performed
in C. See https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KPO02BvAu6g
Both Mark Knopfler and his acoustic guitarist played the song in G with the capo 
on the 5th fret, which is how
I've done it here.
NOTES:
  *   The "G" chord played over the last syllable of the chorus ("-parte") is 
actually the 1st chord of the
      following instrumental.
  **  The last line of the outro slows down.
