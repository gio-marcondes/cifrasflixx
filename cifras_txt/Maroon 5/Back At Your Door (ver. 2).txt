This song is pretty simple, but there are some slightly tricky subtleties.  Almost
 Beatlesque.
 
Tuning: Standard
 
Intro: B7, Bb7sus, Bb, D#
Note: I would emphasize the "middle four" strings on these chords since these are 
the
ones that seem to be played on the song.

Note: The timing is really important here.  Chords should be strummed on that 
exact

of nothing.
